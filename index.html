ABOUT

"DCS, the pioneer in New Digital Craft, seamlessly integrates additive manufacturing and computational design expertise to craft sustainable building components. By maintaining control over both design and production, we ensure the highest quality outcomes. Our offerings include 3D Printed Formworks for tailored Concrete Solutions, Digital Stones crafted from Low Carbon Concrete, innovative Non-standard Concrete Stairs, Functional Shading & Light Guiding Facade Elements, Space Dividers, Large Format Screens, bespoke 3D Printed Tiles, and personalized Art & Architecture solutions."
